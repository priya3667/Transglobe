Student Services

Career Counselling

We support you in making proper career decisions and the right direction to your journey abroad. Free and confidential counselling sessions are provided to students along with their parents for studying abroad and the destination countries opted for. One to one counselling for personal guidance to help choose courses that perfectly fit your career and personal goals. No two people are the same, so why should their consultations be.

Test Preparations

To pursue a course abroad, the students should have a good score of the basic language tests.One of the important steps while planning to study abroad is to appear for certain competitive examinations based on your study destination and university criterias. Trans Globe facilitates coaching services for the below listed exams: IELTS PTE SAT GMAT GRE TOEFL​

`
University Admission Process

Discover the right course for the best career amongst different academic courses from the world class universities around the world. Get unbiased information on all the courses and steer in the right direction. From application process to followups to track movement, we give utmost attention. We also have the latest Scholarships and Bursary updates from the Universities and help you to shortlist courses with maximum benefits.

Funds And Visa Assistance

Students opt for an Education Loan and we do have a good rapport with most of the trustworthy banks that ease off this process. The essential part of the entire procedure is the Financial aspects involved with the student’s Visa process. Our Visa Team is equipped with the most experienced advisers who are well versed with the documentation process and the visa requirements of a specific country.

Visa Applications

We also provide services for their dependent and or spouse visa applications which are thoroughly processed by us which makes it easier for students too.We consult you to know and understand yourself and the world of work in order to make career, educational and life decisions.

Pre & Post Departure Services

Pre Departure Briefings for all students and parents for commencing their studies abroad are essential and we ensure to do that. A rich orientation makes everything easy and smooth! The students get an option to exchange Cash, Traveler's Cheque, or Forex Cards at the most compatible rates. You can also look for temporary accommodations, travel arrangements too with us.
